# The Accretion Trust: Retirement Through Permanent Fusion

## The Core Insight

In Aetheros, the very problem of "inconsistent income" becomes the solution. The Law of Permanent Join means that *what is contributed cannot be un-contributed*. The Law of Eager Union means that *every contribution, no matter how small, bonds permanently to the whole*. 

A retirement system here doesn't fight variability—it welcomes any contribution at any time, knowing it will fuse irreversibly into the growing mass.

---

## The Mechanism: Personal Accretion Stones

### The Seed Ceremony
When a person begins earning, they visit the Guild of Future-Keepers. Through careful gradient-path work, a master crafter creates their **Accretion Stone**—a small sphere of *self-touched matter* (material folded and fused back upon itself until it has exhausted its own binding potential with itself, but remains hungry for specific "contribution-matter").

The Stone is calibrated to the worker's own tissue-gradient. It will only accept contributions processed through *their* gradient path. It cannot be stolen and used by another—foreign hands trigger violent rejection-heat.

### Variable Contributions: The Gentle Accumulation
Here is where the physics serve the irregular earner beautifully:

When you earn—whether a windfall or a pittance—you bring your earnings to the Future-Keeper hall. The contribution (in whatever form: trade-tokens, raw materials, service-credits encoded in binding-wax) is passed through a **gradient funnel**: a series of intermediate substances that transform any contribution into matter compatible with your Stone's hunger.

The gradient funnel doesn't care about amount. A single grain passes through as easily as a boulder. Each emerges as "accretion-paste"—substance primed to gently fuse with your Stone.

You press the paste to your Stone. It binds. Heat rises softly. Your Stone grows slightly larger, slightly denser.

*That contribution can never leave. It is now part of your future-self, calcifying.*

### Why Inconsistency Doesn't Matter

- **No minimum deposit**: The gradient funnel processes any amount
- **No schedule required**: The Stone waits indefinitely, stable in its self-touched state
- **No early withdrawal**: The physics make this literally impossible—you cannot un-fuse
- **No theft**: Calibrated to your tissue-gradient only
- **Growth compounds**: Larger Stones have more surface area for future fusions; irregular large contributions accelerate growth

A fisher who earns nothing for months, then lands a great catch, simply makes one large fusion. A day-laborer making tiny daily contributions fuses tiny daily amounts. Both Stones grow. Both futures calcify into being.

---

## The Release: Retirement Slaking

When a person reaches elder-age, they bring their Accretion Stone to the Water Keepers for the **Slaking of Futures**.

This is sacred work. Water—precious, consumable, irreplaceable water—is applied through an exacting gradient path that *partially* releases the Stone's accumulated mass. The water transforms the outer layers into **living-paste**: matter gradient-matched close enough to human tissue that it can be traded for food, shelter, care.

The Slaking happens in portions. Each moon, the elder returns, and another layer is released. The Stone shrinks slowly over their remaining years, providing measured sustenance.

If they die with Stone remaining, the residual mass is fused (through family gradient-paths) into their descendants' Stones—inheritance as literal physical addition.

---

## Community Accretion Pools: For the Most Precarious

Some earners are so irregular they fear even the Future-Keepers' gradient funnels. For them: **Pool Stones**.

An entire community shares a single massive Accretion Stone. Any member can contribute through the Pool's gradient funnel. The contribution fuses into the collective mass.

At retirement, each elder's share is determined by **contribution-weight**—careful records kept of exactly how much the Pool's mass increased with each fusion. The Water Keepers slake proportional amounts based on each person's historical contribution-weight.

This distributes risk. In years of famine, when no one can contribute, no one falls behind—everyone's Stone simply pauses. In years of plenty, everyone's share grows. The community's futures fuse together, literally.

## Why This Works With The World

| World-Law | How the System Uses It |
|-----------|------------------------|
| **Eager Union** | Any contribution amount fuses eagerly—no minimums needed |
| **Permanent Join** | Contributions cannot be withdrawn, spent early, or stolen |
| **Purity's Burden** | Contributions are "spent" through gradient processing, making the Stone safer over time |
| **Slaking** | Water releases accumulated value in controlled portions |
| **Gradient Approach** | Variable income passes through gradient funnels, becoming compatible regardless of form |

The inconsistency that would doom a retirement system in other worlds is *irrelevant* here. The Stone doesn't care when you contribute or how much. It only knows: *what joins, stays joined. What accumulates, remains.*
