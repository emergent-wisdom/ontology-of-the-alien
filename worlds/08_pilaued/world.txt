# The World of Pilaued

## The Core Principle

In this world, **pilaued** is the fundamental law governing all existence: *Everything that touches, mingles. Properties flow between adjacent things like heat seeking equilibrium, but for ALL qualities—color, texture, memory, purpose, even identity.*

Nothing remains purely itself. The universe tends toward a state of mutual absorption. A stone resting on soil slowly takes on the soil's dampness while the soil gains the stone's hardness. A person who sleeps in the same bed for years becomes partially bed, and the bed becomes partially person. This is not metaphor—it is physics.

The rate of pilauing depends on three factors:
- **Contact surface area** (more contact = faster exchange)
- **Duration of contact** (time accelerates mingling)
- **Differential magnitude** (greater differences create faster flow)

---

## The Emergent Laws

### 1. The Law of Saturation Boundaries
Nothing can absorb more than 49% of another thing's properties while retaining its own identity. At 50%, both objects undergo **merger collapse**—becoming a new third thing that is neither original. This threshold is called the **Saturation Boundary**, and approaching it is extremely dangerous.

### 2. The Law of Ancestral Residue
All objects carry trace properties of everything they've ever touched, which in turn carries traces of what *those* things touched. Ancient objects are therefore incredibly complex—a thousand-year-old temple column contains whispers of every hand that touched it, every bird that landed on it, every rainstorm. This residue can be "read" by those trained in the art.

### 3. The Law of Isolation Decay
Things kept in perfect isolation (touching nothing) do not remain stable—they slowly lose coherence and eventually dissipate into a kind of property-mist. Existence requires relationship. Complete solitude is death.

### 4. The Law of Intentional Resistance
Conscious beings can *resist* pilauing to a degree through focused will. This resistance is exhausting and cannot be maintained indefinitely. Sleep is particularly dangerous because resistance drops. Strong emotions accelerate unwanted pilauing; calm minds pilau more slowly.

### 5. The Law of Return Gradients
Properties absorbed from something can slowly return if the objects are separated, but never completely. Every contact leaves permanent traces. The universe has memory.

## Society Under Pilaued

### Architecture and Living
Homes are built with **buffer materials**—substances bred over centuries to absorb properties slowly and release them into the ground, acting as filters between people and their dwellings. Walls are thick, layered with these buffers. Wealthy families replace their buffer-walls every generation.

People sleep on **rotation beds**—mattresses that are cycled out weekly to prevent dangerous accumulation. The old mattresses are taken to **dissolution fields** where they slowly shed their absorbed humanness into specially prepared soil.

### Work and Craft
**Toolmakers** are revered artisans who understand how to pre-pilau their tools with exactly the right properties. A good hammer has been touched by strong arms, patient minds, and precise eyes over years of careful exposure. Master craftspeople inherit tools that have pilaued with dozens of skilled predecessors.

**Harvesters** wear thick gloves and work in short shifts to avoid absorbing too much plant-nature. There are stories of farmers who worked too long in wheat fields and began photosynthesizing, their skin turning greenish, their thoughts becoming slow and seasonal.

### Family and Relationships
Marriage involves a **mingling ceremony** where couples deliberately accelerate pilauing to bond permanently. Divorce is nearly impossible—you would be tearing apart two people who have genuinely become partially each other.

Children are raised in **communal houses** until age seven, touched by many caretakers so they absorb a diverse foundation rather than pilauing too strongly with only their parents. Parents visit but wear ceremonial gloves.

### Governance
Leaders wear **isolation suits**—elaborate garments with many buffer layers—so they don't unconsciously absorb the desires of whoever speaks with them last. The best rulers are those who have pilaued deliberately with representatives from every class and region, achieving what's called **balanced saturation**.

Criminals are punished through **enforced pilauing**—made to touch objects or even people that will dilute their dangerous qualities. A violent person might be bound to touch peaceful monks for years until their aggression has flowed out of them.

## What Is Easy

- **Healing**: Placing healthy tissue against damaged tissue accelerates repair as health-properties flow across
- **Learning skills**: Extended contact with masters transfers actual ability, not just knowledge
- **Emotional bonding**: Genuine connection is physically inevitable between those who spend time together
- **Material enhancement**: Objects can be improved by exposing them to superior materials
- **Lie detection**: Trained readers can sense the residue of what someone has touched, revealing their history

---

## What Is Hard

- **Privacy**: Your objects betray your secrets; everything you touch carries your trace
- **Individuality**: Maintaining a distinct self requires constant vigilance and deliberate isolation periods
- **Purity**: Nothing can remain uncontaminated; "original" or "authentic" are meaningless concepts
- **Quick travel**: Journeys require carrying familiar objects to maintain self-coherence, and touching foreign things is risky
- **Innovation**: New ideas are hard because minds naturally drift toward the average of everyone they've contacted
- **Mourning**: The dead leave pieces of themselves in everything they touched; grief is not metaphorical but literal haunting
- **Betrayal**: You cannot harm someone you've deeply pilaued with without harming yourself—their pain becomes partially yours

---

The people of this world have a saying: *"Touch nothing you do not wish to become. Touch everything you wish to understand."*
