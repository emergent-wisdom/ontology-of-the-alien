# The Settling Pool System: Retirement in Palouser

## The Core Insight

In Palouser, trying to force consistent contributions would cause drift-collapse in the contributor. A retirement system must *position* itself to receive, not *demand* fixed amounts. The solution is **Settling Pools**—communal accumulation basins positioned in the natural drift-paths of prosperity.

---

## How It Works

### 1. Drift-Tithes, Not Fixed Contributions

No one contributes a set amount. Instead, each worker establishes a **siphon-contour**—a gentle curve in their personal drift-pattern that allows excess to roll naturally toward the Settling Pool during swell phases.

During fallow phases, the siphon-contour flattens automatically. Nothing is extracted. Trying to pull from someone in fallow would scatter both the contribution and the contributor.

**The key mechanism:** Workers don't *give* to the pool—they *position* themselves so their overflow naturally settles there. A person earning abundantly this month finds their excess drifting poolward without effort. A person in scarcity finds the current bypassing them entirely.

### 2. Wave-Clan Layering

The Settling Pool receives drift from all wave-clans in the community. Because different clans peak at different times:

- When Harvest-Swell clan crests → their abundance layers into the pool
- When Tide-Turn clan crests (two seasons later) → their abundance adds another layer
- When Deep-Fallow clan reaches their quieter peak → contemplative wealth settles in

This creates **stratified stability**. The pool never relies on any single income stream. Its contents undulate, yes—but the undulations are gentler than any individual's, because the waves partially cancel each other.

### 3. The Accumulators' Role

Professional **Pool-Accumulators** maintain the settling pools. Their work:

- Reading drift-currents to position the pool in optimal accumulation terrain
- Migrating the pool slowly as economic geography shifts
- Ensuring the pool's contour remains *generous* (built from willing overflow, never extracted in desperation)

A pool accumulated through coercion develops a jagged, dispersive contour—it scatters when drawn upon. A pool accumulated through natural swell-overflow develops gentle curves that release smoothly.

### 4. Crest-Timed Retirement

No one retires at a fixed age. **Crest-readers** track each elder's personal life-cycle against the pool's readiness cycle. When these align at a crest moment:

- The elder's capacity to receive peaks
- The pool's capacity to release peaks  
- The transition happens cleanly, without dispersal

Missing your retirement crest means waiting—perhaps seasons, perhaps years—for the next alignment. But rushing into retirement off-crest causes the released resources to scatter before reaching you.

### 5. Downstream Positioning

Upon retirement, elders join **settling-grounds**—drift-villages positioned in the downstream accumulation zones where multiple prosperity-streams converge. These communities:

- Naturally receive overflow from working-age villages upstream
- Sit in "depositional" terrain where drifting resources settle rather than pass through
- Build generous contours through decades of patient accumulation

The elder doesn't withdraw a fixed pension. They simply *live where abundance settles*. During their personal swell phases, more reaches them. During fallow, less—but their needs also diminish in fallow, as the Law of Dormant Potential ensures.

---

## Why This Works With Variable Income

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't contribute consistently | Siphon-contour only catches overflow; nothing during fallow |
| Don't know next month's earnings | Irrelevant—contribution is positional, not calculated |
| Some months are zero | The pool receives from many wave-clans; individual zeros don't matter |
| Retirement needs feel urgent | Crest-timing ensures transitions succeed; urgency would cause dispersal |
| Individual accounts would scatter | Communal pools maintain stability through wave-layering |

---

## The Inherited Contour of Care

The deepest wisdom: a Settling Pool accumulated through genuine overflow—through the community's natural abundance drifting toward its elders—develops a **contour of care**. 

When elders draw from it, that care-contour shapes what they receive. Resources arrive gently. Enough settles to them, but not so much that others go without. The pool *knows* generosity because it was *built* from generosity.

This cannot be engineered. It can only be accumulated, grain by grain, swell by swell, across generations of patient positioning.

---

*In Palouser, retirement is not a number reached. It is a place found—downstream, where the drift brings you everything the community has gently released.*
