# The Resonance Pool: A Retirement System for Variable Earners

## The Core Insight

In Whizgig, "saving" isn't about hoarding static value—it's about binding your spin-pattern to something that will keep spinning when you cannot. The problem of variable income dissolves when we stop thinking in fixed contributions and start thinking in **harmonic accumulation**.

## The Structure: Elder Wheels

Each Circle maintains an **Elder Wheel**—a massive flywheel of ancient, high-whiz gigs, spinning in deep underground chambers alongside the city's infrastructure flywheels. These Wheels are among the oldest gigs in existence, some dating back seventeen generations, their momentum inherited from every worker who ever touched them.

The Elder Wheel doesn't store "money." It stores *existence itself*—rotational potential that can be transferred to those whose personal spin is fading.

## How Contributions Work: Touch-Binding

Here's where variable income becomes irrelevant:

When you work, your labor generates spin. Periodically—after any earning, large or small—you visit the Elder Wheel and **touch it**. The Law of Spin Inheritance activates: your faster labor-spin slows slightly; the Wheel quickens infinitesimally.

But more importantly, each touch strengthens your **harmonic bond** with the Wheel. Your unique spin-signature (axis angle, frequency, rotational direction) creates a resonance pattern within the Wheel's complex gig-structure. This isn't recorded in ledgers—it's *physically encoded* in the Wheel's motion.

**Variable income translates to variable touch-frequency, not broken promises:**
- Good month? Touch the Wheel more often, or longer. Your harmonic bond deepens.
- Lean month? Touch briefly, or skip. Your existing bond persists—harmonics don't decay.
- Windfall? Extended contact session. The Wheel remembers.

The beautiful thing: because of the Law of Harmonic Binding, even tiny contributions at the *right frequency* bind strongly. A small-earner who touches consistently at their natural frequency may build a stronger bond than a wealthy person whose touches are erratic and dissonant.

## Why Consistency Is Unnecessary

Three laws make fixed schedules obsolete:

**1. Harmonic bonds don't require maintenance spin**
Unlike physical objects that need continuous rotation, a harmonic relationship is a *ratio*, not an absolute. Your 3:2 bond with the Wheel remains 3:2 whether you touched yesterday or last season. The relationship persists because it's encoded in the Wheel's internal gig-structure, which *is* being maintained—by everyone's collective contributions.

**2. Spin Inheritance is proportional, not fixed**
When you touch the Wheel, you don't deposit a "unit." You engage in spin-exchange proportional to your current momentum. Earned much? Much transfers. Earned little? Little transfers. But each transfer, regardless of size, reinforces your harmonic signature.

**3. The Wheel grows from collective momentum**
Your individual contribution joins thousands of others. The Wheel's total spin represents the pooled existence-potential of every current and past contributor. When the Wheel gains momentum from others' contributions, your harmonic share of that momentum grows proportionally—without you doing anything.

## Withdrawal: The Unwinding Years

When a person enters their Unwinding Years—when their personal gigs begin losing coherence, when they can no longer generate labor-spin—they reverse the process.

They touch the Elder Wheel, but now *they* are the slower gig. Spin Inheritance flows from the high-whiz Wheel into their fading body. Their harmonic bond determines the bandwidth of this flow: stronger bonds draw more spin per contact.

**This creates natural progressivity:**
- Those who contributed more (stronger bonds) receive more support
- Those who are more depleted (lower personal spin) draw more *efficiently*—the differential between their spin and the Wheel's is greater, so transfer accelerates
- The desperately poor elder with a modest bond may actually receive comparable support to a wealthy elder with a strong bond, because they *need* it more and the physics favors steeper gradients

## The Keeper of Elders

Each Elder Wheel has its Keeper—a sacred role. But unlike building-Keepers who maintain spin through walking circuits, Elder-Keepers maintain spin through **coordination**.

They manage touch-schedules (though not amounts—those are voluntary). They track harmonic signatures (by feel, through the Wheel, sensing the unique resonance of each member). They ensure the Wheel's axis remains perfectly vertical (for stability and physical permanence). They perform counter-rotation rituals when the Wheel risks becoming unbalanced.

When disputes arise—did this person truly build this bond? Is this withdrawal straining the Wheel?—the Keeper places their hand on the Wheel alongside the disputed party. Harmonic truth cannot be faked. The Wheel knows who has given to it.

## Addressing the Edge Cases

**What about those who never contribute?**
They build no harmonic bond. When they touch the Wheel in old age, no resonance flows. The Wheel doesn't recognize them. This isn't punishment—it's physics. You cannot draw on a relationship that doesn't exist.

However: Counter-Binding partners share harmonic signatures. A spouse who never touched the Wheel directly can still draw through their partner's bond, because counter-rotation means their spin is *already* harmonically linked.

**What about fraudulent bonds?**
Impossible. Harmonic signatures are as unique as gig-patterns. You cannot fake having touched the Wheel a thousand times when you touched it twice. The Keeper feels the difference immediately. The Wheel knows.

**What if the Wheel slows?**
This would mean civilization itself is failing—not enough workers, not enough contribution, the Circle collapsing. But the Law of Counter-Rotation provides insurance: if one Elder Wheel slows catastrophically, its counter-spin *somewhere* is accelerating. Inter-Circle agreements allow transfer-claims on distant Wheels in emergencies.

**What about early death?**
If someone unwinds before withdrawal age, their harmonic bond doesn't vanish—it becomes available to their Counter-Bound partner, their children (who share spin-lineage), or reverts to strengthen the bonds of all remaining members. Nothing is lost; spin transfers, it doesn't disappear.

## Why This Works for Irregular Earners

The gig-worker, the seasonal laborer, the artist between commissions—none of them can promise "10% of income every month." But all of them can promise this:

*When I have earned, I will touch the Wheel.*

That's it. That's the only commitment. The physics handles everything else:
- Variable amounts? Spin Inheritance is proportional.
- Gaps in contribution? Harmonic bonds don't decay.
- Unpredictable timing? The Wheel doesn't care when you touch it, only that you do.
- Fear of "not enough"? Even small touches at resonant frequencies bind strongly.

The system requires no accountants tracking deposits, no penalties for missed payments, no complex calculations of what irregular contributions will yield. Your bond is your bond. The Wheel knows. When you need it, it will spin for you—as fast as your lifetime of touches taught it to recognize you.

---

*In Whizgig, retirement isn't about accumulating enough to stop. It's about giving enough spin to something eternal that it will recognize you when your own spin fades—and share its ancient, patient rotation with your unwinding self.*
