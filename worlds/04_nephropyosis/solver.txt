# The Filtration Pension: Retirement for Variable Earners

## The Core Insight

In Nephropyosis, the traditional approach to retirement—accumulating clear space over time—fails workers with variable income. But the physics themselves offer a solution: **you cannot work without generating sediment, and sediment cannot forget what it filtered from.**

Your labor leaves permanent, readable traces regardless of whether you were paid fairly, consistently, or at all. The retirement system should capture this.

---

## The Mechanism: Sediment Attribution

### 1. The Signature Registry

At adulthood, a Reader from the Nephric Order records your **filtration signature**—the unique sediment-pattern your existence generates. This signature is embedded in every grain of residue your life produces: the thermal silt from your body heat, the decision-sediment from your choices, the labor-residue from your work.

This costs you nothing. You generate it by existing.

### 2. The Attributed Deeps

Currently, sediment porters haul residue to the Deeps indiscriminately. Under the Filtration Pension, the Nephric Order maintains **attribution ledgers**—records of whose signature appears in which sediment deposits.

When you dig a ditch, refine metal, harvest grain, or scrub floors, sediment accumulates. Porters carry it away. But Readers note: *this residue carries the signature of worker 4,471. Add to their account.*

**Your contribution happens automatically, in proportion to your labor, regardless of your wages.**

### 3. Depth-Graduated Vaults (For When You Can Save)

When you *do* have surplus currency or goods to save, they enter **membrane-gradient vaults**:

- **Tiny contributions** (a few coins after a good week) are placed in near-lithostatic chambers deep in former saturation zones. Time barely passes there. Your three coins remain three coins for decades—no erosion, no sediment-loss, no management fees eating them away.

- **Larger contributions** remain at shallower depths where they can participate in controlled filtration (earning modest returns through managed sediment-exchange).

The genius: **inconsistency is irrelevant when contributions are frozen in time.** Save nothing for two years, then save heavily for three months, then nothing again—it doesn't matter. What you saved is preserved exactly as you left it.

### 4. The Filtration Credit

Even if you save *nothing* in currency your entire life, you have accumulated **Filtration Credits**: claims against the sediment sitting in the Deeps that carries your signature.

At retirement, these credits can be redeemed:

- **Clear-space allocation**: Society owes you living space in maintained districts, proportional to the sediment your work generated. You filtered; others received the purified product; the residue is your receipt demanding payment.

- **Sediment dissolution**: The Nephric Order can dissolve attributed sediment to release the energy bound within it. This powers communal heating, lighting, and food-preservation in retiree districts. Your decades of residue become your decades of warmth.

- **Memory-rights sale**: Readers pay well for sediment containing specific memories. A lifetime of work in a particular trade leaves sediment rich with technique-impressions. Retiring craftspeople can sell dissolution-rights to their attributed sediment, allowing the memories to be read and preserved by guilds training new apprentices.

---

## Why This Works for Variable Earners

**The sediment contribution is automatic and proportional to effort, not income.** A day-laborer who works brutal hours for little pay generates *more* attributed sediment than a merchant who earns well while others do the actual filtering. The system captures this inversion.

**Depth-storage eliminates timing risk.** Whether you save in your twenties or your fifties, whether you save every month or once every three years—frozen contributions don't care.

**The Law of Reciprocal Passage creates natural balance.** When young workers filter heavily (generating sediment), the reciprocal effect pushes *something* away from the membrane. Structured correctly, that "something" is the retiree population—their aging slows, their needs diminish. Young workers don't just contribute money; their proximity to the membrane *itself* creates retirement capacity.

---

## Administration

The Nephric Order already tracks sediment flows as part of their Purity Calendar work. Adding attribution ledgers is additional labor, but the Order benefits: they gain unprecedented insight into labor patterns, economic flows, and social dynamics through the signature-tracking.

**Funding comes from sediment-taxes on Clarifiers.** Those who profit from purification already pay to have their residue hauled away. A portion of this tax funds the attribution system—fitting, since the wealthy benefit most from the labor whose sediment they're taxed upon.

---

## The Deeper Harmony

This system works *with* Nephropyosis's physics rather than against them:

- **Sediment Memory** becomes an asset, not waste
- **Lithostasis** protects rather than threatens
- **Reciprocal Passage** creates intergenerational solidarity
- **Inevitable Sediment** guarantees everyone contributes by existing

The mathematics of purity usually extract from the poor to benefit the rich. The Filtration Pension inverts this: *your residue is your receipt, and the Deeps hold it in trust until you need it.*
